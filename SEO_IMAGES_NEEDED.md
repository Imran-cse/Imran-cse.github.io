Please create and add the following images to the `/public` directory for optimal SEO:

## Required Images:

1. **og-image.jpg** (1200x630px)

   - Open Graph image for social media sharing
   - Should feature your name, title, and portfolio branding

2. **apple-touch-icon.png** (180x180px)

   - Apple device home screen icon

3. **favicon-32x32.png** (32x32px)

   - Standard favicon for browsers

4. **favicon-16x16.png** (16x16px)

   - Small favicon for browsers

5. **android-chrome-192x192.png** (192x192px)

   - Android device icon

6. **android-chrome-512x512.png** (512x512px)

   - High-resolution Android icon

7. **profile-image.jpg** (400x400px)
   - Your professional photo for structured data

- Use your brand colors: #0f172a (dark) and #5eead4 (teal)
